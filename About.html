About Te Puia

Te Puia is New Zealand's geothermal wonderland, featuring dramatic geysers, bubbling mud pools, traditional Māori carvers and weavers at their national schools, and the fascinating Te Whakarewarewa geothermal valley. Our mission is to preserve and celebrate Māori culture, art, and traditions, ensuring that they are passed down through the generations.

Visitors can witness the power and beauty of geothermal activity while learning about Māori culture through engaging, interactive experiences. Te Puia's commitment to sustainability and cultural preservation makes it a unique and vital part of New Zealand's heritage.
